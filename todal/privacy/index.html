Privacy Policy

Todal Multimodal

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Todal Multimodal (“Todal,” “the app”) is built to work entirely on your device. This policy explains what the app does and doesn’t do with your data.

What Todal collects

Location. Todal uses your device’s precise location, including while the app is in the background, to detect when you’ve arrived near a bus stop you’ve chosen to monitor, so it can play an audio announcement. This is the app’s core feature and the only reason it asks for location access.

Todal does not send your location anywhere. All location processing — detecting when you’re near a stop, matching that against your saved stops and routes — happens on your device. Your location is never transmitted to Todal’s developer, to the transit agencies, or to any third party.

What Todal does not collect

Todal does not have user accounts or sign-in. It does not collect your name, email, contacts, photos (beyond ones you optionally attach to a saved place, which stay on-device), device identifiers, or usage analytics. There is no advertising, and no analytics or crash-reporting SDK.

Data stored on your device

Your saved places, monitored stops, lines of interest, trip profiles, and ride history are stored locally on your device. None of it is backed up to a server Todal’s developer operates. (If you have iCloud device backup enabled, Apple may include this app’s local data in your personal iCloud backup — that’s Apple’s backup system, not something Todal does itself.)

Network requests Todal makes

To show live bus arrival times, Todal fetches public real-time transit data (GTFS-realtime feeds) directly from the relevant transit agency’s public servers (GoRaleigh, GoTriangle, GoCary). These are anonymous, unauthenticated requests for the whole public feed — your location, identity, or which stops you’re watching are never included in these requests.

Address search and map display use Apple’s MapKit, subject to Apple’s own privacy practices (see Apple’s privacy policy).

Children’s privacy

Todal is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children, consistent with not collecting personal data from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change and the new version will be posted at this same URL.
