Super 8 & 35mm · Raleigh, North Carolina

See What I Offer

I'm a Raleigh-based film hobbyist who develops and digitizes small-format film out of a home setup. If you've got a roll of black-and-white Super 8 or 35mm film to develop, or old Super 8 movie film, negatives, or slides to scan, I can help.

Honest heads-up: this is a passion project, not a professional lab. Expect care, fair prices, and a real person to talk to — not lab-grade turnaround or guarantees. Perfect for personal archives, experiments, and getting your shots back without the big-lab cost.
