Friday

5:00 leave Raleigh.

6:30 arrive at Ayman's house to pickup trailer:
Address: 1908 Hunterwoods Dr, High Point, NC 27265.

7:30 arrive at Oak Hollow campground.
Site A2.

8:00 go to grocery store to get dinner.

9:00 cook dinner, go to bed early.

Back

Saturday

6:00 Wake up early, leave camp site. Breakfast on the road.

9:00 Arrive at Beech Mountain with trailer. Park in lot 3, in the very back.

Option 1

Buy a Beech Day ticket at the window. $82.

1:30 leave to Grandfather campground.
Site B20.

2:15 Drop off trailer, go to Dunn's Deli for lunch.

3:15 Back to Beech Mountain.

4:00 Chill, snowboard, have some beers.

7:00 Dinner.

8:00 Too Many Zoos concert.

10:00 Back to Grandfather campground..

Option 2

Buy a Full Day ticket at the window. $70

12:00 Lunch at Beech.

5:00 leave to Grandfather campground.
Site B20.

6:00 Evening at campsite. Make a fire, cook dinner, drink.
Go to Lowes Foods for dinner and beers. Firewood at campsite or Lowes.

Back
