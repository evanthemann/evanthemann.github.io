Connecting Renaissance Park to Carolina Pines Avenue

Urban exploration

Renaissance Park and Carolina Pines are two popular neighborhoods in the South Raleigh area. Carolina Pines boasts an impressive Civitan Park, which hosts a dog park, baseball fields, tennis courts, volleyball courts, picnic shelters, and a community center with a gym. Renaissance Park has a pool, community center, and an appealing, landscaped, clean, and well-maintained outdoor environment. Each has it's own personality and style. Renaissance has cookie-cutter homes, Carolina Pines houses and yards vary wildly. Renaissance Park, has higher-priced homes, Carolina Pines has some more affordable housing. While different in those regards, they share some qualities. Both are close to downtown, catering to a demographic who enjoy what the heart of Downtown Raleigh has to offer.

Looking on Google Maps, the there are two ways to get from one to the other: Via Tryon Road or via S Saunders Street.

I found a different way!
