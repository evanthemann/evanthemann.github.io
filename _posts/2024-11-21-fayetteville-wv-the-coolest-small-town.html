Fayetteville, WV Trip Highlights

Our vlog from the time I went with Jess 😊: Weekend in Fayetteville, WV | Vlog (youtube.com)

Breweries

Freefolk Brewery: The Freefolk Brewery Taproom – Google Maps

Bridge Brew Works (cool brewery with outdoor-only seating): Bridge Brew Works – Google Maps

Outdoor Activities

Arrowhead Bike Farm and Handlebar & Grill: Arrowhead Bike Farm and Campground – Google Maps

  • Bike rentals (short, easy mountain bike ride to Long Point Overlook, ~2 mi out/2 mi back, also suitable for walking/running)

Restaurants

Pies and Pints: Pies & Pints – Fayetteville, WV – Google Maps

Secret Sandwich Society: Secret Sandwich Society – Google Maps

Burrito Bar at Breeze Hill: The Burrito Bar At Breeze Hill – Google Maps

Tudor’s Biscuit World (amazing greasy biscuits): Tudor’s Biscuit World – Google Maps

Southside Junction Tap House: Southside Junction Tap House – Google Maps

Wood Iron Eatery (We had a nice brunch here): Wood Iron Eatery – Google Maps

Cathedral Cafe (Old church converted to café right downtown – great breakfast): Cathedral Cafe – Google Maps

Parks and Trails

Fayetteville Town Park: Fayetteville Town Park – Google Maps

Park Loop Trailhead: Park Loop Trailhead – Google Maps

Long Point Trailhead: Long Point Trailhead – Google Maps

Endless Wall Trail: Endless Wall Trail – Google Maps

Diamond Point Overlook (part of Endless Wall Trail): Diamond Point Overlook – Google Maps (Loop trail with 2 parking lots and a road in between, about a 5k for the full loop. Amazing views at Diamond Point overlook. You’ll recognize it when you see the sign and a short 100-yard hike out to it. Don’t mistake the smaller vistas for this one.)

Kaymoor Trail: Kaymoor Trail (U.S. National Park Service) (I have not done it, but it seems interesting)

Other Points of Interest

Water Stone Outdoors (THE local outdoors shop in town with beer taps inside): Water Stone Outdoors – Google Maps

Thurmond Station (Historic abandoned town, now part of the park, a bit far to get to): Thurmond Station – Google Maps

Stone Cliff Campground (Cool dispersed campground near Thurmond, although I haven’t camped there): Stone Cliff Campground – Google Maps

Canyon Rim Visitor Center: Canyon Rim Visitor Center – Google Maps (Visitor center with a great view of the bridge, about 150 stairs down to a better view, with an indoor area)
