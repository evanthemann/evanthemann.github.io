How I bike to work.

May is Bike Month in Raleigh, and a city-wide contest has driven me (well, biked me) to bike as much as possible. I work in downtown Raleigh, and I live pretty close to downtown. There are several ways to get downtown, but I wanted a route that was safe, fast, and easily doable most days.
