About Me

My professional journey began in film school, where I learned the art of visual storytelling. From there, I moved into internal communications at a global software company, helping teams connect through video, messaging, and strategic communication. That foundation led me into digital marketing and marketing operations, where I discovered a passion for combining data, systems, and creativity to drive growth. Today, I work as a Marketing Automation Manager at Prometheus Group. In this role, I focus on building scalable marketing operations, supporting go-to-market strategy, and ensuring the right leads, messages, and insights reach the business at the right time.
