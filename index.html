ABOUT ME

My passion is making videos. I provide the most value by taking a real puzzle of an interview and turning it into a cohesive story. I have a corporate style of high-quality subtleties over flashiness. I like to edit most but I also enjoy cinematography. I like to shoot everything I edit when possible. My current job as a Senior Communications Specialist at SAS Software requires me to do a lot more than just video production and video editing. My other responsibilities include project management, writing, web administration, web design, graphic design and asset management.

This website is one of many places to find me on the web. My favorite thing is my Edit with Evan channel (which I don't upkeep as well as I should, nor do I have good organization, but I like it). Check out my YouTube for my current personal stuff or Vimeo for formal stuff.
